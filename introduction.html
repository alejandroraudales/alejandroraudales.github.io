ALEJANDRO RAUDALES

Watching movies had always intrigued me in a way I didn’t understand when I was younger. It was aside from the fact of the action, comedy, or adventure inscribed in the films; it was how they were made. Animation is described as using images and drawings to create the illusion of movement. It has progressed from when it was first introduced as the flat 2D, to the vivid 3-Dimensional form. My thoughts and ideas of what I wanted to be has changed numerous times, but I feel as if the creation of films has always been there waiting for its moment to be noticed.
